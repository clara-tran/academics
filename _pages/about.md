<h1>About me</h1>

I am currently an MA student in the <a href="https://www.mcgill.ca/linguistics" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Department of Linguistics</a> at <a href="https://www.mcgill.ca/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">McGill University</a> (Montréal, QC, Canada). Prior to this, I completed my undergraduate studies in the <a href="https://www.smu.ca/linguistics/index.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Program in Linguistics</a> at <a href="https://www.smu.ca/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Saint Mary's University</a> (Halifax, NS, Canada).

I am interested in the second language acquisition of syntax and in experimental methods for studying linguistic data. My work has focused on Vietnamese learners of English. I am also drawn to sociolinguistics, especially how language is used and talked about online. When I’m not working, I’m usually cooking, baking, or catching up on sleep.

<h1>About me</h1>

I am currently an MA student in the <a href="https://www.mcgill.ca/linguistics" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Department of Linguistics </a> at <a href="https://www.mcgill.ca/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">McGill University</a> (Montréal, QC, Canada). Prior to this, I completed my undergraduate in the <a href="https://www.smu.ca/linguistics/index.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Program in Linguistics</a> at <a href="https://www.smu.ca/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Saint Mary's University</a> (Halifax, NS, Canada).

I am primarily interested in second language acquisition of syntactic phenomena and experimental methods for linguistic data. My target population so far has been Vietnamese learners of English. I also find sociolinguistics intriguing, as I spend (a lot of) time observing Internet language and people's opinions about language. If you don't see me at work, I might be at home cooking/baking or sleeping.

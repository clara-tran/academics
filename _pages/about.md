I am primarily interested in second language acquisition of syntactic phenomena and experimental methods for linguistic data. My target population so far has been Vietnamese learners of English. I also find sociolinguistics intriguing, as I spend (a lot of) time observing Internet language and people's opinions about language. If you don't see me at work, I might be at home cooking/baking or sleeping.
